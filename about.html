Our Story

Modern Web Creators was born during one of the world’s most challenging times — the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020. While the world was shutting down, two passionate minds — Aditya, a sharp and detail-obsessed coder, and Krish, a visionary with a creative and strategic eye — came together with one goal in mind: to bring the online revolution to everyone, from small shops to big enterprises. .

Krish manages everything that happens behind the scenes — from marketing and strategy to client communication — while Aditya brings every website to life with clean, efficient code and innovative features. Together, we’ve built more than just websites — we’ve built digital experiences that empower businesses. .

Today, Modern Web Creators stands as a growing web development company that’s now embracing AI technologies to take website building to the next level. Whether it's a personal portfolio or a full-fledged company website, we are here to deliver quality and affordability to all. .
